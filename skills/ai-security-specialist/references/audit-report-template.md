## Status Definitions

- ✅ **Pass** — All items in this checkpoint verified and working
- ⚠️ **Needs Work** — Some items fixed, at least one remaining open item
- ❌ **Fail** — Critical issues found, not yet resolved
- 🔒 **Cleared for Launch** — All checkpoints pass or only Low priority items remain
- ⚠️ **Conditional** — Medium priority items remain, use judgment before launching
- 🚫 **Not Cleared** — High or Critical items remain unresolved
